Music Mixer Concept

Mermaid Music Mixer will be a deep see inspired music mixing game that incorporates cartoonish and bright styles.

Almost all of the icons including the sounds will be presented by deep sea animals and sea related things such as a fish, whale, crab, coral, seaweed and more.

Users will be prompted to drag each sound into a slot, in which the music mixer will play on a ten second loop. 

Each sound will be aquatic inspired, tying in rhythms and beats to one of the sound icons (Each sound will give the same vibe as that animal)

The design will incororate a colour scheme of warm blues, greens and purples. 

The mixer will feature an illustration of a mermaid who is going to "mix" your songs for you
